About Human Development Index

What is HDI?

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a composite statistic developed by the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) to measure a country's overall development.

Health

Measured using Life Expectancy at Birth.

Income

Project Objective

This application predicts the Human Development category (Very High, High, Medium, or Low) using Machine Learning. It helps governments, researchers, students, and organizations analyze a country's development.
